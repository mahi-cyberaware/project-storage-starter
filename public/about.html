About Project Storage

Learn about our mission, features, and technology stack

Our Mission

To provide a simple, secure, and accessible file storage solution for developers, students, and professionals. We believe everyone should have access to reliable file storage without complexity.

Key Features

Secure Storage

Files are encrypted and stored securely with automatic backups.

Cross-Platform

Access your files from any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile.

Fast Uploads

Optimized upload system supporting large files up to 100MB.

Smart Search

Find files quickly with our advanced search and filtering.

Easy Download

Download files with one click on any device.

Auto-Sync

Files are automatically synchronized across all your devices.
